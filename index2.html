Spongebob Squarepants

About Spongebob

SpongeBob SquarePants is the title character and protagonist of the American animated television series of the same name. He is voiced by actor and comedian Tom Kenny and first appeared on television in the series' pilot episode "Help Wanted" on May 1, 1999.
